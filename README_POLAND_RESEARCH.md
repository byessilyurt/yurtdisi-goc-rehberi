## ğŸš¨ Critical Information

### Must Know Before Applying

1. **Address Registration (Meldunek)**
   - CRITICAL: Must do within 30 days of arrival
   - Location: Part 2, First Week Checklist
   - Without it: Can't get PESEL, bank account, or residence permit

2. **PESEL Number**
   - Your Polish ID number
   - Automatic with address registration
   - Needed for: Everything (bank, SIM, work, healthcare)

3. **Residence Permit Application**
   - Deadline: 45 days after D visa arrival
   - Processing: 4-12 months
   - Cost: 440 PLN (3,520 TRY)
   - Legal stay during processing: YES

4. **Special Turkish Rights**
   - Decision 1/80 gives progressive work rights
   - After 1 year: Renew with same employer
   - After 3 years: Work any employer (same profession)

5. **Dual Citizenship**
   - Poland ALLOWS dual citizenship
   - Can keep Turkish passport when naturalizing
   - Citizenship after 10 years (or 8 years with PR)
